#define LTOA_BUFFER_SIZE   12

The size of the string buffer for the "ltoa" (long to ascii) conversion.

A positive long can be 2^31 - 1 = 2147483647, i.e., 10 characters long. Including the minus sign and a terminating '\0', the size of 12 is sufficient.
